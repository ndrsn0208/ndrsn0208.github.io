My research lives at the seam where statistical learning meets symbolic structure. I am interested in how an agent — human, machine, or otherwise — accumulates skills over years without each new task quietly erasing the last. Most of my recent papers attack this from three angles: bounding the geometry of weight updates, learning compositional concept primitives, and using generative models as exploration scaffolds for reinforcement learning.
